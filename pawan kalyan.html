pawan kalyan

Pawan Kalyan was born as Konidela Kalyan Kumar[39] on 2 September 1971 in a Telugu family to Konidela Venkata Rao and Anjana Devi in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh. Venkata Rao worked as an excise police constable and was transferred on a regular basis. Kalyan was educated at the St. Joseph's High School in Nellore and later in Madras (now Chennai).
