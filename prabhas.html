prabhas

Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju "p…æabhas", born 23 October 1979, known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who predominantly works in Telugu cinema. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015.
